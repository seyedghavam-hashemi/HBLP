The Advantages of Previsualization

The first advantage of previsualization is to visualize complex movie sequences before starting the process of production. Previsualization is essential when we talk about cost and time saving and its aid in the planning process, it improves the communication between producers ,directors and their crew, also improves the communication with the studios what they are putting their money into. Previsualization can provide other advantages, such as It’s used to map out how the visual effects (VFX) will fit into an otherwise live-action scene,Creating previsualization not only can save films and television series and shows valuable time and money on set but also in post-production.
